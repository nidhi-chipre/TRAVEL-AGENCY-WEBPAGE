TOP 3 DESTINATIONS

DUBAI,UAE

The United Arab Emirates, sometimes simply called the Emirates, is a country in Western Asia located at the eastern end of the Arabian Peninsula. It borders Oman and Saudi Arabia, and has maritime borders in the Persian Gulf with Qatar and Iran.

INDIA

The massive development of tourism in India comes as no surprise. The country has 36 World Heritage sites and 103 national parks. Tourists in India can visit the Taj Mahal in Agra, the hill forts in Rajasthan and the holy city of Varanasi, plus jungles and exotic wildlife

HONG KONG

You can explore up to 260 islands including Lantau, Lamma, Cheung Chau, and Peng Chau. There are many ferries and the world famous star ferry also run here. The view of the city from the water is breathtaking. Take a Spa day:Tourism in Hong Kong is complete when you haven't had a day in the spa.
